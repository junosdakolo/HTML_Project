Biography

Dakolo Emmanuel Imomotimi was born on the 7th day of May 1994 in Nigeria. He attained his High School Education At the Nigeria Navy Secondary School Calabar in 2012 and in 2013, he proceeded for his Undergraduate degree at the University of Cebu in Philippines where he graduated with a degree in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering in 2017. After graduation , He proceeded to study 3D Modelling and Animation for Games and Movies. He holds a PGD in Cloud Computing from the University of Texas at Austin. In 2018, he worked as a Junior Naval Architect at the Naval Shipyard Port Harcourt. in 2020 he was employeed as a Marine Surveyor with the Nigerian Maritime Administration and Safety Agency (NIMASA) where he currently work.
